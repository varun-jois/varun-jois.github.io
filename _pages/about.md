PhD candidate in Computer Science specialising in computer vision and 
generative AI for faces. My research focuses on the use of reference 
images to improve face-related tasks such as super-resolution, video 
enhancement and facial expression transfer. I have 4 peer-reviewed 
publications. I have experience across the full model building pipeline 
including data collection, model design, training and deployment, with 
a focus on building practical systems that run efficiently on constrained 
hardware. Expected graduation August 2026.
